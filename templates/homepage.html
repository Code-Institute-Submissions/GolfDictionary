Welcome to The Goltionary.

The Golftionary is a community driven site designed for beginners and those wanting to know more about the wonderful sport of golf can discover information about some of the often confusing terminology used in golf.

If you are know a bit about a term used in golf and it is not on the site, feel free to add it by using the link in the menu on the side.

Sadly only registered members are allowed to post or edit definitions. Luckily registering is super simple and can be done with the links in the menu.
